About Us

We are a team of university students and our capstone project, Credit Score Prediction, is designed to help individuals assess their financial health by providing an accurate and data-driven credit score estimate.

We Are CreScore

Understanding credit scores can be complex, and many people struggle to identify the factors influencing their creditworthiness. Our project leverages Machine Learning (Random Forest Algorithm) to analyze multiple financial parameters such as income, debt, payment history, credit utilization, and credit inquiries to provide users with a reliable prediction of their credit score.

By combining Machine Learning and Data Analytics, our project aims to empower users with actionable insights to improve their credit worthiness.

Why Choose Us

Accuracy & Reliability

Unlike generic online credit score calculators, our model leverages Machine Learning (Random Forest Algorithm), which analyzes real-world credit patterns and historical data to provide a highly accurate credit score estimate.
